About Me

A sneak peek into my life

I am an American Army brat who was born in Paris (just hours after Princess Diana was killed, interestingly). Most of my childhood was spent traveling and attending different schools around the world. I finally settled down here in Austin last summer as a social worker, alongside my beautiful fiancée and our soon-to-be daughter!

In my free time I love to play golf with the boys and volunteer my services to the Grace Covenant Church. There's nothing I love more than worship and the opportunity to meet new people who share my passions! Additionally, everything in this about section is absolutely untruthful.
